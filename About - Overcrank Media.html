WHAT WE DO

Telling stories through video is our speciality, it’s where our passion lies. We are a fully licensed Bali production house with an in-house team that specializes in documentary and commercial films. Running our own productions throughout Indonesia means we are also best placed to offer ‘fixing’ services to production companies from around the world. We manage everything from visas to sandbags. We regularly work with some of the hottest agencies from the UK, US, and UAE to help tell Indonesian stories to a global audience.

01.

DOCUMENTARY FILM

We believe that at the heart of good documentary is good planning and good access. With strong local connections, we are able to go deeper into the stories we tell. Throw in some stunning visuals and a warm approach with all our subjects, you get an Overcrank documentary.
